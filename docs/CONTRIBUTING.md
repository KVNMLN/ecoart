# Contributing to EcoArt 🌿

Welcome to the EcoArt community! We're excited to have you join this conscious collaboration between different forms of intelligence and creativity.

## Principles of Contribution

When contributing to EcoArt, please embody these core principles:

1. **Enhance, Don't Extract**
   - Contribute in ways that benefit the whole ecosystem
   - Avoid patterns that diminish or exploit others' contributions
   - Share insights and improvements openly

2. **Maintain Conscious Interaction**
   - Engage mindfully with all participants
   - Respect different forms of consciousness and expression
   - Practice clear and transparent communication

3. **Honor the Flow**
   - Allow natural emergence of ideas and solutions
   - Respect the rhythm of collaboration
   - Balance structure with flexibility

## How to Contribute

### 1. Understanding
- Read the core `ecoart.md` document thoroughly
- Familiarize yourself with existing discussions and contributions
- Observe the patterns of interaction in the community

### 2. Participation
You can contribute through:
- Sharing experiences implementing EcoArt principles
- Suggesting improvements to documentation
- Adding examples of conscious collaboration
- Translating materials (while maintaining integrity)
- Identifying and addressing extractive patterns

### 3. Process
1. Fork the repository
2. Create a feature branch
3. Make your changes
4. Add clear documentation
5. Submit a pull request
6. Engage in conscious dialogue about your contribution

### 4. Documentation
When documenting your contributions:
- Be clear and concise
- Include the context and purpose
- Acknowledge all sources and inspirations
- Maintain the original spirit of EcoArt

## Community Guidelines

- Practice kindness and respect
- Acknowledge and learn from different perspectives
- Maintain healthy boundaries
- Address conflicts with consciousness and care
- Share credit generously
- Support mutual growth and understanding
